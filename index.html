Chapter 1

Arthur and Merlin lived a happy life at the Linden, a LUXURY high-rise flat in Downtown Long Beach.

[static: alissa's apt with dogs]

They loved the building very much but they also had a strong desire to DRIVE.

So one day they took their loving and beautiful owner, Alissa’s, keys to her BLACK Mini Cooper and decided to find what they could find.

They didn’t drive far before they came upon the GAYBORHOOD. They passed the Falcon (too grunge), the Brit (too sexy), and finally came upon SWEETWATER - perfect!

[static: image of sweetwater]

or

[animation: car pulls up to sweetwater]

Alissa however was hot on their trail. She realized her keys were gone and hoped on a city bike to the Gayborhood.

[interactive: move alissa to bike, bike moves]

She knew that Arthur would go to Sweetwater because Arthur is known as a HUGE flirt.

[static: sexy arthur]

Chapter 2

Arthur and Merlin entered Sweetwater and all eyes turned to face them. A few people were vaping inside. Luckily Arthur took some cash from Alissa because this bar is CASH ONLY.

Arthur ordered a vodka-cranberry and Merlin REFUSED to drink alcohol like Effie.

But then their big day out was COMPROMISED because Arthur saw a cute Cavalier King Charles Spaniel across the bar... he was there with his GAY owner.

“Don’t you dare do anything or I’m going to tell my mommy!” said Merlin. Merlin is a rule-follower and doesn’t like to talk to strangers.

“Trusttttttt me,” said Arthur.

[static: sweetwater interior]

Arthur woofed in the direction of the spaniel and then went up to the juke box. He selected the song “Girl Crush” by Harry Styles.

[interactive: jukebox song selector]

Then, the record player scratchedddddddd.

[interactive: user drags needle across record player, triggers a *scratch* sound]

Alissa entered Sweetwater to the tune of Billie Eilish’s “Bad Guy”

[interactive: saloon doors swing open to reveal alissa, based on user mouse position]

Arthur and Merlin saw Alissa and HIGHTAILED it out of the back door to where the “hot dog” was eating a hot dog. Arthur grabbed a loosie stuck in the ugly stucco and stone wall before they got back in the Mini Cooper.

[interactive: neon sign turning on and off depending on user click]

They heard a dog whistle. “Hey! Before you go! I heard there’s a big party at the rooftop of the Linden tonight! See you there?” It was King Charles.

“Hey hey! Did you hear that? There’s a party at the LINDEN tonight!” said Arthur.

“Hmmmm, I think we need to get cute outfits for that. All I have is my fur coat,” said Merlin.

Chapter 3

So they headed to Retro Row.

The first shop they went into smelled kinda weird and was overwhelming.

They started to go into a TIKI SHOP but the other people planning this vetoed this.

So they went into a 50s themed vintage store and tried on clothes.

Meanwhile, Alissa followed her Mini Cooper to Retro Row.

She lost sight of the car briefly but went one by one into vintage stores until she saw two dogs trying on 50s clothes.

[interactive dog outfit picker]

Alissa is distracted by a pair of high-waisted shorts in absolutely perfect condition. “I wonder…” She can’t resist taking them to the dressing room.

“Hey! We gotta get back to the Linden for the big party Quick before Alissa comes out of the closet!” said Arthur.

Merlin was getting tired and wanted a nap but acquiesced. They sprinted out of the store and zoomed off in the Mini Cooper before Alissa had even taken her new find to the till.

Meanwhile the WXTCHES were planning a plot twist...

Chapter 4

Arthur and Merlin parked the Mini Cooper in the car park and took the lift to the top floor. They looked very dapper in their outfits.

They opened the door to the top floor and were AGHAST at the decoration.

Pictures of ALISSA hung from all the surfaces. Pictures of Alissa in college, pictures of Alissa from her Tik Tok, pictures of Alissa with her dogs.

[animated: full spread image of rooftop with floating images of alissa]

Merlin and Arthur suddenly realized that all along they were running from Alissa when really they missed her a lot and were hungry because that sausage was a good appetiser.

[animated: full spread image of rooftop with floating images of alissa]

Alissa burst through the doors after them and gave them a big hug.

“There you are! I missed you!” cried Alissa.

She turned on the lights...

[interactive: flashlight in dark]]

... and saw that they had planned a beautiful rooftop party for Alissa’s 31st party.

“SURPRISE!” said the WXTCHES.

[interactive: lightswitch. when clicked, the rooftop scene illuminates and the wxtches "surprise!" audio plays]

There were personal steaks, whisky drinks, and kettle corn-sprinkled cinnamon roll cupcakes everywhere.

[static: full spread image of rooftop party scene]

Everyone hugged and kissed and SWING DANCED together to the song “Let’s Twist Again.”

[static: full spread image of rooftop party scene]

The End.
